Design clean, modern **Login and Signup screens** for a SaaS platform called **GolfGive**.

⚡ BRAND IDEA:
GolfGive is a platform where users track golf scores, win rewards, and support charities. The design should feel premium, trustworthy, and purpose-driven.

---

🧠 BRANDING:

* Logo text: "GolfGive"
* Optional tagline:
  "Play. Win. Give Back."

Place logo at top-left or top-center of the auth card.

---

🎨 LAYOUT:

Split screen layout:

LEFT SIDE (Brand Story Panel):

* Headline:
  "Play with Purpose"
* Subtext:
  "Track your game, win rewards, and support causes that matter."
* Background:
  Deep green gradient (#0B3D2E → #145A41)
* Optional:
  Subtle pattern or abstract texture

---

RIGHT SIDE (Auth Form Card):

* Clean white or light background
* Card with soft shadow and rounded corners

---

🔐 LOGIN PAGE:

Title:
"Welcome back to GolfGive"

Fields:

* Email
* Password

Options:

* Remember me
* Forgot password

CTA:
"Log In"

Footer:
"New to GolfGive? Create an account"

---

📝 SIGNUP PAGE:

Title:
"Create your GolfGive account"

Fields:

* Full Name
* Email
* Password
* Confirm Password

Checkbox:
"I agree to the terms and privacy policy"

CTA:
"Create Account"

Footer:
"Already have an account? Log in"

---

✨ UX DETAILS:

* Password visibility toggle (eye icon)
* Inline error messages
* Button loading state
* Smooth transition between login and signup

---

🎯 POST-AUTH FLOW:

After signup:
Show message:
"Welcome to GolfGive — let’s choose your impact"

→ Redirect to Charity Selection Page

After login:
→ Redirect to Dashboard

---

🎨 STYLE:

* Clean SaaS design
* No glassmorphism
* Strong typography hierarchy
* Consistent with rest of product

---

🚀 GOAL:
Create authentication screens that feel like a real product, aligned with GolfGive’s identity — combining performance, rewards, and impact.
